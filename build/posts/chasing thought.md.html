chasing thought

Sat Apr 10 2021
I’m convinced the thought that fills my mind most of the time is just a complex of reactions to the strange world I’m living in. Creative urges struggle to redirect my mind to what I don’t see, to what might be.

I want to deal with what’s possible, not wallow is what’s already taken place and left its ugly mark.
